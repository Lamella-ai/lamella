What's fine

  • Forking the repository on GitHub.

  • Mentioning Lamella in a blog post, a talk, or a tutorial.
  • Saying "I use Lamella for my books" in any context.
  • Including the Lamella name in a list of tools you use.
  • Internal use of the unmodified software at your company.

What needs permission
