Owned by

Chart accounts scaffold as Expenses:<Entity>:Vehicle:<slug>:Fuel, etc. If the vehicle is personal, pick Personal.

Display name vs. slug

Display name is what shows in pickers and reports — year + make + model reads well. Slug is the Beancount path segment; auto-derived if blank, must start with a capital letter.

VIN + plate are optional but useful for downstream lookups (insurance, tolls, parking citations).

All optional. GVWR > 6,000 lb lifts §280F luxury-auto caps; placed in service may differ from purchase date; fuel type drives EV/PHEV credit eligibility.
