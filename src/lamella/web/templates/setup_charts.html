Setup: expense charts

Every entity with a tax schedule (Schedule C business, Schedule F farm, Personal / Schedule A) needs its required expense categories open in the ledger. Without them, the AI classifier has to fall back to generic accounts, and Schedule C line mappings are incomplete. Scaffolding is idempotent — already-open categories are skipped.
