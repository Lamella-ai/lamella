Setup: import-rewrite detection

Scan of your chart for paths that don't follow the canonical Root:<Entity>:… shape. Common causes: ledgers imported from another Beancount setup, ad-hoc "Custom" paths, or short buckets like Expenses:Bills (no entity segment).

Detection-only. This page lists what would be rewritten and how often each path is used — no files are touched. Automated rewrites are intentionally not live: moving historical postings across accounts is destructive and often requires your judgment on which target to pick (e.g., which of your two Fabrikam Suvs a generic "FabrikamFuel" posting belongs to).
