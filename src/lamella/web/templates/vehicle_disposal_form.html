Bookkeeping mode

The gain/loss figure is arithmetic on the numbers you enter. Ordinary-income (§1245 recapture) vs. capital-gain characterization belongs to your tax professional.

Type

Sale, trade-in, total-loss (insurance payout), gift, scrap. The type changes which downstream worksheet rows the system pre-populates but doesn’t fork the ledger postings.

Numbers

Adjusted basis defaults to the most recent election basis (or cost basis if no elections have been recorded).

Accumulated depreciation is the sum of §179 + bonus + MACRS taken over the vehicle’s life — user-entered, since the system doesn’t compute MACRS schedules.

Gain/loss = proceeds − (basis − depreciation).
