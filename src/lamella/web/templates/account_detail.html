Use this for accounts where you know the balance as of a specific date but don't have the full pre-ledger transaction history. The anchor records "this account had X on this date" as a reference point. Drift between consecutive anchors and the postings between them surfaces in the audit report — gaps stay visible instead of being silently padded over.
